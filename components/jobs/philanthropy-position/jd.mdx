[Hack Club](https://hackclub.com) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that supports high school coders in starting coding clubs, hackathons, and building awesome projects. Founded in 2014, Hack Club's mission is to support, inspire and motivate teenagers from all backgrounds to become highly-technical, and use their computer skills to solve problems.

Hack Club is a fun, quickly-growing start-up, run by highly-creative engineers, building programs every day for thousands of teenagers from around the world. Watch a video of our summer train trip <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2BID8_pGuqA> to learn more about Hack Club, and to see the sort of projects you would be supporting. Highly technical Silicon Valley founders donate to Hack Club, and we want to sustain and grow that network. The first $5 million has been raised from a dozen committed donors, and the right candidate can leverage our network to build a sustainable $5m-$7m a year donor network. 

We are looking for an experienced head of philanthropy, however we also invite more junior people to apply (details below).  

The ideal candidate can devise a philanthropy strategy, and lead on executing it. They'll be excited to play a pivotal role in scaling Hack Club to reach hundreds of schools across America. They can demonstrate strong, persuasive writing skills, creative communications, a strategic mindset, and a deeply-held passion for creating opportunity for teenagers from all backgrounds. Actual work includes writing emails, proposals, buildign and maintaining a donor system, working with designers and filmmakers, crafting messaging and listening. Hack Club is a lot of fun.
